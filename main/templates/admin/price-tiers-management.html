How Price Tiers Work

Price tiers are automatically determined based on the average price of selected vehicle (brand + model + variant + year).

Example: If a car's average price is RM 45,000, it will automatically be assigned to the "Mid-range (RM 20,000 - RM 60,000)" tier.
